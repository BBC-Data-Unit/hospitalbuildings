            East And North Hertfordshire NHS Trust

            The cost of general acute hospital repairs backlogs at East And North Hertfordshire NHS Trust has risen 56% from £26,749,752 to £41,772,163 in the last financial year, according to analysis of data released by NHS Digital.

            The backlog of high-risk repair costs has, however, fallen 87.4% from £4,321,717 to £545,846.

            At a national level the high risk repairs backlog has risen 26.6% to £2,363,023,974, while the overall repairs backlog has risen 9.8% to £11,637,971,550 (figures adjusted for inflation).

            In 2022-23, there were 0 “clinical service incidents caused by estates and infrastructure failure,” remained unchanged of NaN% on 2021-22, when there were 0 such incidents.

            Clinical service incidents are defined as those leading to “services being delayed, cancelled or otherwise interfered with owing to problems or failures related to the estates and infrastructure failure.” Incidents include problems with electrical, water or ventilation systems, internal fabric and fixtures, roofs and structures, or lifts and hoists.
